We Are SMS

Stock Management System - SMS - is an opensource online Inventory Management system.

SMS also offers services of an online store such as adding products to a cart and a paperless billing system.

This project is made opensource with the view to reduce the shedding of tree due to paper used in maintaining stock levels and printing bills.
